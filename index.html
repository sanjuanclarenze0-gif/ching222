Introduction

Spider-Man is a popular fictional superhero from Marvel Comics, co-created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko. He debuted in Amazing Fantasy #15 in August 1962. His secret identity is Peter Parker, a teenager from Queens, New York, who gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider.


Origin and Personality

Origin: Peter Parker gains superhuman strength, agility, the ability to cling to walls, and a spider-sense after a spider bite.


Motto: The iconic phrase "With great power comes great responsibility" guides him after the death of his Uncle Ben.


Personality: Spider-Man is known for being a relatable, intelligent, and sometimes awkward character who juggles his superhero life with typical teenage problems.
